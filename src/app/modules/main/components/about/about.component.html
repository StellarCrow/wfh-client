About us
Who we are?

We are small indie game company located in Kyiv, Ukraine. This is our first (out of many) games to come.

Established in 2020 in underground labs of EPAM, we are PFH - Play From Home.

Our desire is to help You spend your free time with your friends or even help You make new ones during the lingering lockdown period.

What is this game?

The game is all about fun.

Draw, write and then conbine it all to create the sickest tee design. Only the most unigue and quirky T-shirt will leave the tournament alive!

This is a game You can enjoy with up to 5 of your friends. Enjoy, smile, laugh, create and remember - your happiness makes the world a prettier place.

How do you play?

- First, you draw something silly and think of a witty remark, both 3 times.

- Then, you are presented with a random set of phrases and drawings your friend made. At this point you will be asked to compose a tee from the components you are given.

Finally, the most anticipated phase of all, the game presents you the funniest tees in a brawl against each other. You must vote for the one you like the most and decide the winner that gets the chiken dinner.
